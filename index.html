About Us

Govt. Polytechnic College Kangra came into existence in the year 1992, with the up gradation of erstwhile Junior Technical School established by Punjab Govt. in 1959. This polytechnic has earned the reputation of being one of the premier institutes of the state by producing and providing technically competent, professionally proficient and socially relevant human resources to cater for the industrial and technological world of work. This Polytechnic has attained the capability and distinction of acquiring ISO 9001-2015 by developing the optimal infrastructure, and becoming a cynosure for the seekers of the technical education . This Polytechnic is imparting three year diploma courses in the emerging areas of technology viz. Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Instrumentation Engineering and read more >> Our Vision Keeping abreast of the latest trends in technology and fast changing scenario in the national and international employability, Government Polytechnic, Kangra has the following vision:- “To produce highly competent technical manpower to match the dynamic environment of the global market, offering services to the industry and community ensuring a place for India in the select band of developed countries”.
